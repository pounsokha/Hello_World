About Ports

Ports are vital to global trade, providing essential services that facilitate the movement of goods and passengers worldwide. They serve as crucial gateways for imports and exports, connecting businesses and consumers across the globe. In this section, we explore the different types of ports and their unique functions.

Key Port Types:

  • Cargo Ports: Handling goods and freight.
  • Passenger Ports: Serving travelers and tourism.
  • Specialized Ports: Catering to unique needs (e.g., fishing ports).
